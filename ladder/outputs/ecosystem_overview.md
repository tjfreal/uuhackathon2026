# Note System

*A local-first knowledge system for building, resurfacing, and communicating what you know.*

A personal knowledge system that lives on your own machine. You capture notes in plain text, and a set of tools helps you revisit old ideas, notice connections, and explain the system clearly to other people.

Notes get captured and forgotten. Search helps you find things you already know to look for. This system is aimed at helping you find things you forgot you knew and making that process teachable to other people.

Everything runs on your machine. No subscription. No cloud account required. Your files stay yours.

The library-as-a-ladder idea started as a people-first approach to help-desk and library service: meet people where they are and help them reach the next rung. This project carries that approach into note-system scaffolds and knowledge work in the automation age.

Ladder is part of the system because explanation is part of the work. If the system cannot explain itself to different kinds of people, it has not finished the job.

## Layers

- **Foundation**: Note System itself — local markdown notes, review habits, and the notes CLI.
- **Retrieval**: Surface and Bridge help you find what matters again, including things you were not already searching for.
- **Organization**: Signals extracts structure from freeform writing so notes can become more usable over time.
- **Sensemaking**: Trails helps reconstruct how an idea developed instead of treating notes as isolated fragments.
- **Communication**: Ladder explains the system in audience-specific terms and preserves the people-first framing behind it.
- **Presentation**: Decksmith turns structured content into editable decks for talks, classes, and demonstrations.

## What exists now

- **Note System**: A local-first markdown-based knowledge system for capturing, reviewing, and resurfacing what you know. (built)
- **Surface**: Resurfaces older notes when they are likely to be relevant again. (proposal)
- **Bridge**: Detects meaningful cross-domain connections across the corpus. (proposal)
- **Signals**: Extracts structured observations from freeform prose so notes become more usable over time. (proposal)
- **Trails**: Reconstructs how an idea developed across time and notes. (proposal)
- **Ladder**: Generates audience-aware explainers for the note-system ecosystem. (in progress)
- **Decksmith**: Generates editable presentation decks from structured content. (proposal)

## Why Ladder belongs here

`Ladder` is the communication layer because a people-first system has to stay explainable. The library as a ladder started as a service principle: meet people where they are and help them reach the next rung. In this ecosystem, that same principle shapes how technical knowledge work gets described, taught, and shared.
