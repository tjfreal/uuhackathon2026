Statement|||The Big Shift|||Use the Julia set as modulation, not silhouette.|||The turning point was realizing that the vase should stay mostly circular on average, while Julia structure acts as a controlled modulation field. That reframed the project from brute-force fractal conversion into guided form design.|||
Title & Bullets|||What Actually Worked|||Ring-based watertight mesh pipeline\nGradual modulation through Z\nSymmetric return toward circularity\nInward-biased deformation around a stable center|||Once the model started behaving this way, the form became much more convincing. The best versions do not drift wildly or flare outward. They keep a stable envelope and use stronger inward pulls to create drama.|||
